# 202512141244 - Technical amendment specification

## Objective

Per blogpost the amount of words in a post should be displayed. This will be part of a reading ux. 
The wordcount should be shown only on a blogpost, outlined to the right of the date, all the way on the right side of blogpost. 

The yaml frontmatter words should be excluded from the count.
